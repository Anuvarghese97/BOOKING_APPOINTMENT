ABOUT US

Care with Cure

City Hospital “A home for the sick people who are treated towards life” The hospital is focused on delivering diligent care to the poorer sections of the society.

Years before, our institution started as a small health centre with 20 beds this slowly grew up to the present. Currently, it is a 100 bedded hospital with Operation theatre, ICU, Laboratory, X-Ray, 24 hours Casualty, Scanning centre and many more. The institution has been working as the cradle for the needy. We are focused to deliver standardized nursing care, nursing education, palliative care and health educations to the residents of this area.

Director’s Message

City Hospital has been committed to excellence of care for sick people irrespective of caste, creed or religion since its existence in 1982. Today health care has grown beyond our imagination. The trends and technologies in health care are transforming medical services at a rapid pace. The challenge is to continue providing quality health care at an affordable cost despite the ever increasing cost of equipments and medical staff.
A team of talented, caring doctors, competent, compassionate nurses, skillful technical and support staff are working together with a holistic approach to provide the best possible medical and nursing care. Our Surgeons are talented and committed, supported by a highly qualified Anesthesia Team. I am sure with well equipped operation theaters, laboratories, imaging facilities, Pharmacy and Critical care Units, City Hospital will provide every patient the very best of medical care.

With best regards,

Mr.Francis M.O.
Director
