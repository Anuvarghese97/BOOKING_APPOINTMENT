Care with Cure

City Hospital “A home for the sick people who are treated towards life” The hospital is focused on delivering diligent care to the poorer sections of the society. Years before, our institution started as a small health centre with 20 beds this slowly grew up to the present. Currently, it is a 100 bedded hospital with Operation theatre, ICU, Laboratory, X-Ray, 24 hours Casualty, Scanning centre and many more. The institution has been working as the cradle for the needy. We are focused to deliver standardized nursing care, nursing education, palliative care and health educations to the residents of this area.
